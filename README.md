## 🏢 The Business Context & Objective
The retail e-commerce sector faces severe challenges with customer loyalty and high acquisition costs. For **Ora E-commerce**, the primary business goal of this project was clear: **Identify customers at risk of churn and propose data-driven retention strategies.**

Instead of just looking at historical sales, the objective was to understand customer behavior, calculate Customer Lifetime Value (CLV), and build a predictive approach to stop revenue leakage before it happens.

## ⚙️ Methodology: CRISP-DM
To ensure a structured and business-oriented approach, the project followed the **CRISP-DM (Cross-Industry Standard Process for Data Mining)** methodology. We moved systematically from Business Understanding and Data Preparation to Modeling, Evaluation, and Deployment through an interactive dashboard.

## 🛠️ The Solution: Power BI Command Center
While the project involved predictive machine learning models (Logistic Regression & XGBoost) to calculate churn probability, my core responsibility was translating these complex outputs into a **Strategic Action Dashboard** in Power BI for the executive team.

### 1. Executive Overview & Sales Performance
A macro-level diagnosis of the company's financial health. I processed transaction data to visualize total revenue, sales by category, and extreme seasonal peaks (such as the massive volume spike in November).

### 2. RFM Customer Segmentation
I developed a dynamic Recency, Frequency, and Monetary (RFM) model. Customers were clustered into highly actionable segments (e.g., *Champions*, *At Risk*, *Hibernating*). This allows the marketing team to stop broad, expensive campaigns and focus budget exactly where it matters.

### 3. Cohort Analysis & Retention Tracking
To understand the speed of customer drop-off, I built a Cohort Analysis matrix. This visual diagnosis tracks user retention month-over-month, proving that post-purchase engagement is critical to maintaining a healthy active user base.

### 4. Actionable Retention Strategies
Bridging Data Science and Marketing. Based on the churn predictions and RFM clusters, I created a dedicated view for strategy execution. The dashboard recommends specific actions per segment:
* **High Churn Risk:** Aggressive discounts and win-back email campaigns.
* **VIP / Champions:** Loyalty programs and early access to new collections.
* **Recent Customers:** Onboarding flows and feedback programs.
